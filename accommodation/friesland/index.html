Friesland Hotel

Cradle Cottages Accommodation Muldersdrift

Friesland Hotel comprises of two bedrooms, each with an en-suite shower, basin and toilet.

The main bedroom has a double bed and the second bedroom two single beds.

The open plan kitchen/dining area is fully equipped with an electric oven and stove, microwave, fridge/freezer, washing machine crockery, cutlery and coffee and tea making facilities.

The lounge has a comfortable sleeper couch and a TV with all the DSTV channels.

On the private patio is also a Weber braai.

A continental breakfast consisting of muesli, oats, rusks, croissant with cheese and jam and yoghurt is also included.
